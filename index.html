The Artist

25+ Years of Breaking Glass

I started on a washing machine. Soldering my first project on a piece of cardboard, on a curved surface, in a laundry room. Not glamorous — but I was completely hooked from that first moment.

Today I work from a converted garage studio in the Wisconsin countryside, creating stained glass, fused glass, and copper foil work that has been years in the making.

Built for Glass Artists

Introducing My Kiln Journal

I built this because I needed it myself — a dedicated place to track firings, schedules, and kiln notes without digging through my messy binder. Now it's available for glass artists everywhere.

  • Save firing schedules, log notes and results for each firing
  • Search and filter firing logs to find exactly what you need
  • Manage multiple kilns and track maintenance info
  • Built by a glass artist, for glass artists
