Why It Exists

Glass is too expensive to guess with

I built My Kiln Journal because I needed it myself. After years of glass work, I had binders full of notes and a memory that couldn't always be trusted. When something went wrong — or went beautifully right — I wanted to be able to go back and find exactly what I did. My Kiln Journal is the tool I wished I had on day one.

✓
Stop wasting glass
Log every firing so you stop repeating expensive mistakes and know exactly what went wrong.
✓
Find what worked
Search and filter your logs by kiln, schedule, tags, or rating. Pull up the exact firing that gave you that result.
✓
Save time
Everything in one place, searchable, always with you. No more digging through binders.
✓
See before & after
Attach photos to each firing log and visually compare results over time.

Installation

Add it to your home screen

My Kiln Journal is a web app — which means there's nothing to download from an app store. Just visit the site and follow these steps to install it on your phone. It will look and feel just like a regular app.

1
Open Safari
On your iPhone or iPad, open the Safari browser. My Kiln Journal must be opened in Safari for the install option to appear — it won't work from Chrome or other browsers on iOS.
Screenshot placeholder
2
Go to mykilnjournal.com
Type mykilnjournal.com into the Safari address bar and visit the site. Make sure the page has fully loaded before the next step.
Screenshot placeholder
3
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a square with an arrow pointing up. A menu will slide up from the bottom of your screen.
Screenshot placeholder
4
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen." You can keep the default name or rename it to whatever you'd like.
Screenshot placeholder
5
Tap "Add" to confirm
Tap Add in the top right corner. My Kiln Journal will appear on your home screen just like any other app. Tap it to open and get started.
Screenshot placeholder

Tip: Once installed, always open My Kiln Journal from your home screen icon — not from Safari. This gives you the full app experience without the browser bar.

1
Open Chrome
On your Android phone or tablet, open Google Chrome. My Kiln Journal works best when installed from Chrome on Android.
Screenshot placeholder
2
Go to mykilnjournal.com
Type mykilnjournal.com into the Chrome address bar and visit the site. Wait for the page to fully load.
Screenshot placeholder
3
Tap the three-dot menu
In the top right corner of Chrome, tap the three dots (⋮) to open the browser menu.
Screenshot placeholder
4
Tap "Add to Home screen"
In the menu, tap "Add to Home screen." Chrome may also show a banner at the bottom of the screen offering to install — you can tap that instead if it appears.
Screenshot placeholder
5
Tap "Install" or "Add"
Confirm by tapping Install or Add. The app icon will appear on your home screen and you're ready to go.
Screenshot placeholder

Tip: Some Android devices may show an automatic "Add to Home Screen" banner at the bottom of Chrome when you visit. You can tap that directly — no need to go through the menu.
